Our Philosophy

Healthcare Helpers is a team of professional Healthcare Advisors who are licensed to provide health and life insurance advice. Healthcare Helper's philosophy is centered around educating each member so they are empowered to make their own decisions. While we will share our frank opinion on which plan option make be in your best interest, it is ultimately you who must decide, without pressure from agents.

A good experience in any relationship requires that we are able to be ourselves while at the same time being able to respect the differences of others. Our success has come from building long term relationships with all our members, so they know they can count on us to be there whenever they need advice or assistance.

Life is difficult. No one goes through life unscathed by the burdens of age, and the loss that each of us will inevitably face as our youth disappears and those we love depart. Despite all of the things that make us distinct and different from one another, there is much more that connects us, and in particular, our shared sufferings.
