5 Sneaky Causes of Weight Gain

1. Portion Control Creep

Even healthy foods can lead to weight gain if eaten in large portions. Pay attention to serving sizes to keep calories in check.

2. Hidden Sugars

Foods marketed as "healthy" can be packed with hidden sugars, especially in items like smoothies and granola. Check the labels!

3. Stress Eating

Chronic stress increases cortisol levels, which can lead to cravings for high-fat, high-sugar foods, adding more calories than you realize.

4. Not Enough Sleep

Lack of sleep can slow metabolism and increase hunger hormones, leading to overeating.

5. Skipping Strength Training

While cardio is great, neglecting strength training can lead to muscle loss, slowing your metabolism. Incorporate weight training to maintain lean mass.

Pay close attention to these sneaky causes and tweak your habits to stay on track!
